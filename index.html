About Me

I'm currently working towards my Ph.D at the University of Texas at Arlington. My main interests are NLP, neural networks, with the occasional web-dev project on the side. I also like keeping track of "Big Data" projects and initiatives that make use of the plethora of data out there available to the public.

There's a lot to learn from data we already have, and computing is one of the ways I think we can improve the quality of life for people; by empowering, teaching, and giving people the tools with which to meaningfully interpret the world around them. I am always looking for new opportunities to be part of a project, or just learn and take in the insight of others.
