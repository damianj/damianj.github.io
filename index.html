About Me

I am currently on my way to finishing my PhD in computer science from the University of Texas at Arlington (UTA). While at UTA have worked as a full-stack developer for the department. I have also worked on the infrastructure of our lab and deployed our own sub-network wihin the greater UTA network which has facilitated inter-server communication and allowed all of our projects to be hosted under one domain. I love learning new things and expanding my knowledge into domains that I have not explored before. Always looking forward to the next challenge.
