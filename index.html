About Me

I'm currently a student at the University of Texas at Arlington. I am working towards my Bachelors in Computer Science and possibly a Masters or PhD down the road. I graduated UT Austin with a biology degree, but my experiences during my last semester led me to make the leap into this field. I did some undergraduate research at UT Austin, which really helped me hone in on what I was passionate about. I love technology and Computer Science as a field. There are so many possibilities today afforded to us by technology, and I know there's much more waiting to be accomplished.

My main interests are algorithms, assistive technology, machine learning, and VR. I also like keeping track of "Big Data" projects and initiatives that make use of the plethora of data out there available to the public. There's a lot to learn from data we already have, and computing is one of the ways I think we can improve the quality of life of people; by empowering, teaching, and giving people the tools with which to meaningfully interpret the world around them. I am always looking for new opportunities to be part of a project, or just learn and take in the insight of others.
